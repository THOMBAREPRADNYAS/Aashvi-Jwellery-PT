PERSONAL DATA COLLECTED

aashvijwellery.com (the “Site”) is owned and run by AASHVI Jwellery Ltd. (“we”, “us” or “our”). Your privacy is taken extremely seriously and steps are taken to ensure your privacy is protected at all times. We also believe it is important to let you know what information is collected from the site and why, and what we do with this information.

At AASHVI, we collect personal information about you in order to provide a unique personalised experience catered to you. This also allows us to alert you to administrational, product or service changes, and for marketing purposes. We gather this information about you when you engage in the following ways:

You register with or subscribe to AashviJwellery.com and marketing channels

You use our online services and place orders

You sign up to our newsletter

You interact with us online via social media, digital advertising on our or any other website where AASHVI are represented

You ask for information, provide a testimonial or feedback, comment on blogs and articles that are represented by AASHVI

You attend an event hosted by us or where we are acting as sponsor

We recommend when you register your personal information with AASHVI, you consent to the use of your personal data in line with AASHVI's Privacy Policy. If you are under the age of 16, we advise you to inform a parent or guardian about our privacy policy and obtain their agreement to this privacy policy before using any of our online services, including, but not limited to, registration with AASHVI, subscribing to our newsletter or placing an order with us.

WHAT PERSONAL DATA IS COLLECTED

At AASHVI we may collect and store information which includes but is not limited to, the following information about you:

* Title * Your Name * Delivery & Billing Address * Phone Number * E-mail Address (Collectively ‘Your Personal Data’).

HOW WE USE 'YOUR PERSONAL DATA'
‘Your Personal Data’ enables us to provide the following:

A unique personalised service on the site which targets information, services, content, experiences relevant to you

To inform you of any administrative, product or service changes

For marketing purposes such as information about the brand, new product lines, or marketing events, etc.

In the event you do not wish for us to use ‘Your Personal Data’ for marketing purposes, please either:

Click on the ‘Unsubscribe’ link in the emails we have sent you (if any).

Contact our Customer Service Team by emailing sales@aashvijwellery.com and if you wish to be removed from our marketing database.

We can assure you, we will never collect personal data about you without your explicit consent. We will retain the data until the time at which you decide to ‘unsubscribe’ (as above). AASHVI will also conduct an annual review of all data held by the company and will remove any data no longer needed. You are able to request to check the information that we hold about you by e-mailing us at sales@aashvijwellery.com, We will provide this information within 40 calendar days of receipt of the information request. If you find any inaccuracies we are happy to delete or correct it promptly. The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.

AASHVI will neither sell your personal data to third parties nor market it elsewhere. Under the company contract with AASHVI employees are duty-bound to respect the confidentiality of your data and abide by the Thailand's Jewellery Retail Codes of Conduct. If you would like to know more information about the personal data we hold about you or make a complaint, please contact sales@aashvijwellery.com in the first instance in order that we can investigate thoroughly. Should you find our response unsatisfactory, you can make a formal complaint with the Information Commissioners Office by calling 7247 6233 23.

RETENTION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We take all the correct measures to ensure that any personal data is kept secure and is kept for only so long as is necessary for the purpose for which it is used. If in any case your personal details change or if you have any queries about how and the way we use your personal data, please inform us at noreenlondon.com or by emailing our Customer Service team at sales@aashvijwellery.com

AUTOMATICALLY RECORDED INFORMATION

When you access the AASHVIJWELLERY website, general non-personal information (In the internet browser used, number of visits, average time spent on site, pages viewed) is recorded automatically (not as part of our website maintenance). This information is only acquired to engage our website's appeal and to enhance its performance, content and functions. The data we collect and acquire is not processed any further nor is it transmitted to any third parties for any use.

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

We are able to reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any material changes we may make in the future to our Privacy Policy will be updated in our Privacy Policy section to view. We would advise to review it occasionally to remain informed and updated for the changes.
