ABOUT AASHVI

UNMISTAKABLY YOU

You are like no other. You are unmistakably you. And Aashvi was created to be the unapologetic expression of you.

Its very name is the perfect evocation of what we are all about – celebrating the whole of you. Aashvi is the name of our founder and creative director, as well as a name meaning Goddess of Art and Knowledge. Because Aashvi's purpose is to create designs to bring out the radiance you have within you. Empowering you to shine your light on yourself and those around you, unapologetically. Just like a diamond.

Being one of a kind, you deserve jewellery that sets you apart. To create a unique identity, Aashvi incorporates our signature diamond star into all her designs. A diamond star shines always and forever, celebrating your own light through both the finest materials and the most powerful symbolism. Because you are worthy of the attention our jewellery brings you. Because a star is what you are.

All of Aashvi’s jewellery is unique. It is made with the highest artistic expression in mind, so it can be a worthy reflection of your life’s journey.

Never apologise. Express who you are.

You are unmistakably you.

Aashvi Jewellery is a Responsible Jewellery brand, who is committed to promoting and protecting ethical, social and environmental practices. All Aashvi suppliers are required to operate within the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme, which requires to follow a strict certification protocol.
